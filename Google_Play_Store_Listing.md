## Short Description (80 char max)


## Full Description (4000 char max)

```
CareerPath is your complete offline-first career guidance platform for students. Whether you're choosing your academic stream, exploring career options, finding the right institutes, or discovering job sectors — everything you need for informed decision-making is in one beautifully designed app.

PERSONALIZED CAREER EXPLORATION
Start with your academic stream — Science, Commerce, or Arts — and get personalized career suggestions tailored to your background. Explore hierarchical career paths from broad categories down to specific specializations. Discover options you might not have considered, bridging the knowledge gap for students in rural and semi-urban areas.

HIERARCHICAL CAREER TREE
Navigate a structured tree of career information: Stream → Category → Sub-Category → Specialization → Full Details. For Science: Engineering (Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil), Medical (MBBS, Biotechnology), Pure Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Research). For Commerce: Accounting (CA, B.Com), Finance (Banking, Investment), Business (Management, Economics). For Arts: Social Sciences (Psychology, Sociology), Law, Design, Languages, Public Services.

COMPREHENSIVE CAREER DETAILS
At each career leaf node, access complete information including:
- Detailed career description and future prospects
- Recommended books with authors and reference links
- Educational institutes with city locations and websites
- Applicable job sectors with descriptions
- Affordability indicators for trust-run institutes and scholarship opportunities

STREAM-BASED PERSONALIZATION
Create your profile with name and stream selection. The Suggestions tab shows relevant career categories immediately. The Explore tab lets you browse all streams freely. Enable affordability preferences to prioritize economically accessible options for students from weaker backgrounds.

OFFLINE-FIRST EXPERIENCE
All core features work without internet — browse careers, view details, and plan your future. Data is stored locally with backup options. Network-aware design with caching and retry mechanisms for smooth experience on slow connections.

BREADCRUMB NAVIGATION
Easy navigation with breadcrumb trails showing your current position in the career hierarchy. Jump back to any level instantly.

ACCESSIBILITY FEATURES
Clean, intuitive UI designed for all students. Expandable sections, URL launching for external links, and responsive design for mobile devices.

Start your career journey today. Download CareerPath and make informed decisions about your academic and professional future.
```

## Privacy Policy

A privacy policy URL is **required** by Google Play. Since the app is offline-first and collects no user data, a simple policy stating the following is sufficient:

- App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data
- All data is stored locally on the user's device
- No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect data
- User can export or delete all data from within the app

> Host this as a simple webpage or a GitHub Pages link and paste the URL in Play Console.
